# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give clear, actionable feedback.
- Help others learn and unblock.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to project maintainers via repository channels.
All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.
